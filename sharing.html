Sharing Locations with Frogjump

Frogjump works with different Android intents in order to share locations. There is (currently) no UI inside of Frogjump itself to share a location.

geo: links

If an application supports sharing links with geo: URIs, as a latitude and longitude, Frogjump can share this type of URL.

In order to use this with OsmAnd from arbitrary map locations:

  1. Press and hold on the location on the map you would like to share. A location bubble will appear.
  2. Press on the location bubble. A menu will appear.
  3. Press Share location.
  4. Press geo:.
  5. Select Frogjump Geo Handler.

Google Maps links

You can click on most Google Maps links from websites and applications, and choose open them with Frogjump.

This may not work if you have set Google Maps for Android as the default handler for Google Maps links. You can revert to a prompting behaviour through Android's settings:

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Press Apps.
  3. Press Maps or Google Maps.
  4. On Android Marshmellow (6.0) and later, press Open by default, Open supported links then select Ask every time.
  5. On Android Lollipop (5.1) and earlier, under Launch by default, press Clear Defaults.

Note: Some Android distributors change the layout of the Android Settings application. Please consult with your distributor's documentation (normally the manufacturer of your device) for more information about accessing these settings. These instructions are for AOSP-based distributions of Android, used on Nexus, Cyanogen and Google Play Edition devices.
