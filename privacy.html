Frogjump Privacy Statement

Locations aren't logged, but groups do not show who is a member of the group. As a result, if someone knows or guesses your Group ID, the locations may leak to third parties.

Google App Engine, Cloud Endpoints, and Cloud Messaging is used to transfer messages between devices.
